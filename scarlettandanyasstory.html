Once upon a time, there lived a happy penguin couple. Their names were Pih and Puh. They were engaged.

Pih had very large eyes, a Santa hat, and always wore a suit. Puh, the bride, bought a gorgeous white dress for her wedding.

She had a tiara with a jewel at the top instead of a veil. She also had bangs and wavy hair. She was nervous for her wedding though.

Puh had three bridemaids. They were named Pah, Peh, and Pough. One of the guests at the wedding was named Crisisa. She was the

ringbearer. On the day of the wedding. Puh had second thoughts. She did npt want to marry Pih! She wanted to live alone. She actually

realized that she hated Pih!
