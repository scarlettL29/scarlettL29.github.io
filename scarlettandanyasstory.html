Once upon a time, there lived a happy penguin couple. Their names were Pih and Puh. They were engaged.

Pih had very large eyes, a Santa hat, and always wore a suit. Puh, the bride, bought a gorgeous white dress for her wedding.

She had a tiara with a jewel at the top instead of a veil. She also had bangs and wavy hair. She was nervous for her wedding though.

Puh had three bridemaids. They were named Pah, Peh, and Pough. One of the guests at the wedding was named Crissisa. She was the

ringbearer. On the day of the wedding, Puh had second thoughts. She did not want to marry Pih! She wanted to live alone. She actually

realized that she hated Pih! Overcome with emotions Puh ran to Crissisa's room, and hoped to find some comfort with her bestie.

Howevery she was brutally awakened, when she saw Crissisa's huge pear shaped bottom falling out of the window.

Puh's eyes widened when she saw he and Pih's diamond wedding-bands squished under the lumpy pile of Crissisa.
