All about me

my hobbies:

Sports:

I have played water polo since third grade. I played for Redwood and I play for SHAQ (my club team). I've also swam since I was really
young and will swim for Redwood in the spring.

Instruments:

I have played the violin since I was 5. I am in the Marin Symphony Youth Orchestra. I also sometimes play the guitar for fun.

Art:

I like to paint but I can't paint people. I enjoy painting landscapes.



My favorite things:

Music:

I like listening to rock and indie music, but I mostly listen to everything. My Spotify wrapped included the artists Solya, Billie Eilish, and the Arctic Monkeys.

Favorite movies

I like action, comedy, and horror movies. My favorite horror movie franchise is Scream, but the newer ones aren't that good. The image to the side is Scream 4, which is the best in my opinion.

Favorite foods

I love spaghetti, rice and salmon. I'm not a very picky eater, so I could eat anything as long as it doesn't have cilantro. I don't like cilantro.
